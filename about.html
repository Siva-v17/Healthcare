Our Mission

We Exist to Heal, Innovate, and Inspire

Founded by Dr. Edwin Park in 1999, MediCore began as a 12-bed clinic with a singular mission: give every patient access to world-class care regardless of their background or means. Today, we operate one of the largest multi-specialty hospital networks in the region.

Our philosophy is simple — great medicine requires both clinical mastery and human connection. We invest equally in our physicians, our technology, and our culture of kindness.
